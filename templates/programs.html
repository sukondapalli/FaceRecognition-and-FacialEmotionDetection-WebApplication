Programs Offered By Us:

Upon contacting Sanctum as your potential treatment provider, you can expect an invitation to receive a tour and consultation with the
professional experts. Our psychiatrists specialize in working therapeutically with individuals that are suffering from depression,
anxiety and much more.

Drug Rehab Program for CEOs

REVIVE provides a highly regarded Executive Rehab program for Business Professionals/CEOs/Executives. Many of the individuals treated by our professional team, have spent their lives developing their careers, working extremely hard and undergoing an inconceivable amount of stress in their professional journey.

Adolescent Addiction Treatment

REVIVE provides personalized residential treatment for teens/adolescent population for their specific requirements such as substance addiction, gaming addiction, depression etc. Our experts help guide and support parents who are worried along with establishing a therapeutic alliance easily with adolescents.

Game Addiction Treatment

The computer and console gaming industry is growing with its biggest forms being Massive Multiplayer Online Role Playing Games (MMORPG). These enable the player to fully immerse themselves in a fake world where they achieve a multitude of tasks. REVIVE helps such individuals to stay away and not be adversely affected.
